Corona Simulation

By Carl, Matthias and Siyu


This simulation gives the user the possibility to decide what is best to stop the spread. Please have a look at the settings, actions and infos and then start the simulation. You can change the settings and take actions during the simulation.

On the window to your left, you can add communities/towns, and control the actions that can be taken to contain the spread of the virus.
You can change the default settings under "Settings". All except the “infection probability” value are correspondent to the actual probabilities. The infection probability is approximated to simulate a realistic spread.
Under the "Actions" tab, you can enact a travel ban, the compulsory wearing of face masks as well as social distancing" All of these regulations can of course be undone.
Under Info, you can find the statistics displaying how many people are in a certain state of an illness.
The reset button leads to a hard reset and restarts the whole simulation as well as all the settings.
